The Sunday Tech Series Season-III:
Session 2: Emerging needs for cyber security

It's our great pleasure to inform you that with the success of Sunday Tech series season II session 1, STME NMIMS Indore conducted its second session of "The sunday Tech Series Season 3" on 26th June 2022, at 4PM IST.

NMIMS Indore fabricated the concept of Sunday Tech Series to keep people equipped with the knowledge of tech related topics and programming languages. The first two seasons of Sunday Tech Series witnessed large and engaging audience participation with over 500+ registrations. The convener of Sunday Tech Series is Dr. Aaquil Bunglowala, Associate Dean, STME NMIMS Indore.

The session started with an intuitive Q/A round conducted by Ms. Darshika Ajmera, and Ms. Aashi, students of STME, NMIMS Indore with our expert Mr Shrikrisha Dikshit who's a cyber security professional at Nangia Andersen LLP. Mr Shrishrisha addressed the doubts of students, and explained them about the major threats in the field of software development, and ways to avoid them.

Mr, Shrijrisha explained us about the Phishing attacks, SQL injection, password attacks, malware attacks etc. Dedication, smart work, and curiosity are the most important qualities required to excel in this field.

The QA session was followed by "Emerging needs for cyber security" session, conducted by Ageya Jha, and Anshi Jain who are the students of BTech, CS BS IV year. Experts firstly explained participants the need of cyber security in IT domain, followed by the live demonstration of some cyber security tools. They also introduced participants to the blockchain technology, and performed a live penetration test. Ethical hacking was later introduced, followed by the scope and career options in cyber security.
